README

This AngularJS app displays weather on your current location via latitude & longitude.
Before showing the weather according to your current position, you must autorize your navigator geolocation.

You have also the possibility to search for current weather by country code or country or zip code.
If you have not autorized your navigator geolocation,completes the form in your right side. Thanks!
